Computer

AI in games enhances gameplay by controlling non-player characters (NPCs), adapting difficulty, and creating immersive experiences. It uses machine learning and algorithms to simulate realistic behaviors, predict player actions, and generate dynamic content.

Step into a thrilling Air Hockey match where the AI plays the role of an elite goalkeeper. As a human player, you’ll need to outsmart the AI, using your creativity and quick reflexes to break through its defense. It’s a fast-paced battle of wits—can you score past the AI’s unbeatable defense?
