Team Of The Tournament — EURO2024

UEFA’s team of technical observers has released the UEFA EURO 2024 Team of the Tournament, featuring six Spain players, two from France and one each from England, Germany and Switzerland.

Kyle Walker
Right-back

Team: England
Walker was a standout performer for England with his explosive pace and solid defending. He provided key defensive stability and was also involved in England’s attack, especially with his overlapping runs and precise crosses. His speed and ability to recover defensively made him an invaluable asset.

Mike Maignan
Goalkeeper

Team: France
Maignan was one of the best goalkeepers of the tournament, making key saves during critical moments, especially in high-pressure knockout matches. His performances ensured that France had a solid foundation at the back and was crucial in their advancement through the tournament.

Manuel Akanji
Center-back

Team: Switzerland
Akanji was the backbone of Switzerland’s defense, consistently putting in solid and disciplined performances throughout the tournament. His ability to read the game, make crucial interceptions, and maintain composure under pressure helped Switzerland hold strong against some of the tournament’s toughest teams.

William Saliba
Center-back

Team: France
Saliba impressed with his composure and leadership in defense, often neutralizing the opposition’s key threats. His physicality, coupled with his ability to distribute the ball effectively, played a key role in France’s defensive solidity and their overall success in the tournament.

Marc Cucurella
Center-back

Team: Spain
Cucurella’s work rate and attacking contributions were exceptional throughout EURO 2024. He consistently provided width on the left flank, making overlapping runs and contributing to Spain’s offensive play. His ability to assist in both attack and defense made him one of Spain’s most consistent players.

Dani Olmo
Attacking Midfielder

Team: Spain
Olmo’s creativity and vision were key to Spain’s attacking play. He consistently provided assists and contributed to the team’s attacking moves. His ability to find space, deliver key passes, and create opportunities for his teammates made him one of the standout midfielders of the tournament.

Fabian Ruiz
Central Midfielder

Team: Spain
Ruiz was instrumental in linking up Spain’s defense and attack. His passing ability and vision helped Spain retain possession and create numerous opportunities. His work off the ball, combined with his ability to break down opposition defenses with his passes, made him a standout performer in the midfield.

Lamine Yamal
Forward

Team: Spain
Yamal’s electrifying pace and dribbling skills made him one of the most exciting players to watch at EURO 2024. His ability to take on defenders and create space for himself and his teammates was pivotal to Spain’s attacking success. Despite his youth, he showed immense maturity and was a game-changer throughout the tournament.

Nico Williams
Winger

Team: Spain
Williams was another exciting young talent who impressed with his pace and direct running. His ability to stretch defenses and create chances from wide positions made him a key contributor to Spain’s attacking play. His dribbling and flair were essential to Spain’s offensive strategies throughout the tournament.
